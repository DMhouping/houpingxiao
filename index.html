Short Biography: I am currently an assistant professor in the Institute for Insight, Robinson College of Business of the Georgia State University. I received my Ph.D. degree from the department of Computer Science and Engineering, The State University of New York at Buffalo (UB) in May 2018. I got my bachelor degrees in Statistics from Beijing Normal University, China.

Research Interests:

  • Data Mining, Machine Learning
  • Business Data Analytics
  • Text Analytics
